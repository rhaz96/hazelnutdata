Wang et al 2012 use 19 total socioeconomic variables to develop a regression model for station level, 2011 ridership of the Nice Ride bike share program in Minneapolis. Notably no weather variables are included. They achieve an adjusted Adjusted \({R^2}\) of approximately 0.87. \({R^2}\)
